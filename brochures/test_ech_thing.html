ECH Models

PRODUCT FEATURES

The ECH model is our 3 Phase, Dual Brake electric chain hoist

LOAD SHEAVE

Deep groove pockets fully support the load chain during operation. This reduces vibration and chain wear as well as providing a true vertical lift.

INVERSE PHASE SEQUENCE PROTECTION DEVICE

Controls the circuit, not allowing the hoist to function if there is wiring or power supply errors.

ALUMINUM BODY

Die cast aluminum body is lightweight with a baked paint finish.

HEAVY DUTY CHAIN CONTAINER

All of our hoists come with a heavy duty chain bag at no extra cost.

FORGED CARBON STEEL HOOKS

Under excessive loads, the hook will open gradually and not fracture. Ball bearings produce a smooth, 360° rotation. Hook safety latches are standard.

HEAT TREATED HELICAL & SPUR GEARS

Precision machined and enclosed in oil bath lubrication, resulting in a quiet, smooth, cool operation and long life.

HEAVY DUTY MOTOR

Increased performance through heavy duty H4 rating as well as standard thermal protection.

PULL-ROTOR MOTOR BRAKE

An extremely durable and advanced design which contains no brake coil to fail, unlike conventional brakes.
